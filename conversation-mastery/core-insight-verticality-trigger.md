# 🎯 Core Insight — Verticality Trigger

## Upgrade in Thinking

Instead of:

> "If they seem open…"

Shift to:

> "If they are **already going slightly vertical on their own**…"

---

## Meaning

Do not *guess* openness.
Do not *assume* readiness.

**Observe behavior.**

Depth is not invited.
It is **joined**.

---

## Practical Rule

- Wait for them to add meaning, emotion, or personal context
- That is a **vertical move**
- Then follow with **+1 depth**

---

## Key Principle

> You are not creating depth.
> You are recognizing and extending it.

---

## Calibration Question

What exactly did they say that made this a vertical move?

Train your perception to answer this in real time.
