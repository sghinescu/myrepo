# 🧠 Lesson 1 — Key Concepts Summary

## Core Ideas

### 1. Horizontal vs Vertical Conversation
- Horizontal = facts, events, surface-level exchange
- Vertical = meaning, reasons, personal experience

### 2. The Signal of Depth
- Not emotion alone
- 👉 Revealed reasoning (WHY) is the strongest signal

### 3. The +1 Depth Rule
- Do not jump topics
- Do not go abstract
- 👉 Take one small step deeper into what was already said

### 4. Follow, Don’t Force
- Don’t make people think
- 👉 Detect when they are already thinking and follow

### 5. Experience > Definition
- Avoid abstract questions
- 👉 Stay inside lived experience

---

## Heuristics

Ask yourself:
- Did they say what happened, or why it mattered?
- Am I going deeper, or just changing direction?

---

## Mental Model

> Emotion = weather 🌦️
> Reasoning = architecture 🏛️

---

## Operational Goal

👉 Track reasoning
👉 Follow it
👉 Expand it slightly (+1 depth)

That’s how conversations become naturally deep.
