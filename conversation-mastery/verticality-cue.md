# 🔍 Verticality Cue — What Signals a Real Move Deeper

## The Question
What **observable cue** shows someone has made a vertical move?

## The Short Answer
Not just facts.

👉 The moment they add **personal meaning**.

---

## What Actually Changes (Mechanically)
A sentence shifts from:
- **What happened** (neutral fact)

to:
- **What it meant / felt like / why it mattered** (personal layer)

---

## Examples

### Horizontal
"I changed jobs recently."

### Vertical
"I changed jobs recently… I couldn’t stand the environment anymore."

**What entered?**
- Emotion (couldn’t stand)
- Evaluation (negative judgment)
- Personal stake (their experience)

---

## The Cue Stack (what to listen for)
You are detecting the arrival of at least one of these:

- 🎭 Emotion (frustrated, excited, relieved)
- 🧠 Meaning ("it felt pointless", "it mattered to me")
- 🎯 Motivation ("I wanted…", "I needed…")
- ⚖️ Evaluation ("it was toxic", "it was amazing")

If one appears → **vertical move detected**

---

## Operational Rule

1. Wait for one cue
2. Reflect it
3. Validate it
4. Go **+1 depth**

---

## Key Principle

> Depth begins the moment reality becomes *personal*.

Your job is not to push there.
Your job is to **notice when they step there first**.
